## Step 4: Safety Tips for Responses

### When Buyers Contact You:

1. **Screen First**
   - Ask: "Are you local to the Bay Area?"
   - Ask: "What's your budget and timeline?"
   - Ask: "Do you have financing or paying cash?"

2. **Only Share Details with Serious Buyers**
   - After screening, share meeting location
   - Never share home address
   - Always meet at public location

3. **Test Drive Protocol**
   - Meet at Tesla Supercharger or police station parking
   - Photo of their driver's license
   - Verify insurance
   - Daytime only
   - Bring a friend

4. **Block Spammers Instantly**

## After the Car Sells:

### Disconnect Google Voice:
1. Go to https://voice.google.com/settings
2. Click "Delete" next to your number
3. Confirm deletion
4. Your real number stays private forever

### Delete Temporary Gmail:
1. Go to https://myaccount.google.com/delete-services-or-account
2. Select "Delete a Google service"
3. Choose Gmail
4. Follow prompts to permanently delete

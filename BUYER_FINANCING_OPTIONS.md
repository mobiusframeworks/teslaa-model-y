# Buyer Financing Options for Your Tesla Model Y

You prefer **cash**, but many serious buyers may need financing. Here's what financing options are available for private party used car sales and the pros/cons for YOU as the seller.

---

## üè¶ FINANCING OPTIONS FOR BUYERS

### 1. **Credit Union Auto Loans** (Most Common for Private Sales)

**How it works:**
- Buyer gets pre-approved from their credit union (or yours - see below)
- Credit union cuts a check directly to you
- You sign over the title
- Clean, simple transaction

**Popular Credit Unions:**
- **PenFed** - 5.49%-17.99% APR, up to 84 months
- **Consumers Credit Union** - Pre-approval online
- **Navy Federal** - Military/family members
- **Local Bay Area Credit Unions** - Often best rates

**Pros for YOU:**
- ‚úÖ You get paid in full immediately (bank check)
- ‚úÖ No risk - you get cash before signing title
- ‚úÖ Common and easy to work with

**Cons for YOU:**
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Buyer needs pre-approval (can take 1-3 days)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è May need to meet at DMV or bank for paperwork

---

### 2. **Bank Auto Loans** (Chase, Wells Fargo, etc.)

**How it works:**
- Similar to credit unions
- Buyer gets pre-approved
- Bank issues check to you
- You transfer title

**Pros for YOU:**
- ‚úÖ Same as credit unions - you get paid immediately
- ‚úÖ Bank checks are safe and reliable

**Cons for YOU:**
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Banks often have higher rates than credit unions (buyer may not qualify)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è More paperwork sometimes

---

### 3. **Online Lenders** (LightStream, MyAutoLoan, etc.)

**How it works:**
- Buyer applies online
- Gets approved in minutes to hours
- Funds deposited to buyer's account
- Buyer pays you via bank transfer or cashier's check

**Popular Online Lenders:**
- **LightStream** (by SunTrust) - 6.99%-25.49% APR
- **MyAutoLoan** - Marketplace of lenders
- **Capital One Auto Navigator**

**Pros for YOU:**
- ‚úÖ Fast approval (same day often)
- ‚úÖ You still get cash immediately

**Cons for YOU:**
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Buyer gets money first, then pays you (slightly more risk)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Higher interest rates = harder for buyer to qualify

---

### 4. **Tesla Financing** (For Used Teslas)

**Does Tesla finance private party sales?**
**NO** - Tesla only finances cars purchased through Tesla.com (their inventory)

**However:**
- Buyer can refinance AFTER buying from you
- Not helpful for the initial purchase

---

### 5. **Third-Party Financing Services** (AutoPay, Caribou, etc.)

**How it works:**
- Services like **AutoPay** or **Caribou** help buyers get loans
- They work with multiple lenders
- Money goes to you as seller

**Pros for YOU:**
- ‚úÖ Handles paperwork
- ‚úÖ You get paid

**Cons for YOU:**
- ‚ö†Ô∏è May charge fees
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Less common

---

## üí∞ WHAT YOU SHOULD DO AS SELLER

### **Option 1: Cash Only (Simplest)**

**Require:**
- Cashier's check or bank check
- Wire transfer
- Cash (meet at bank to verify)

**Benefits:**
- ‚úÖ No waiting for loan approval
- ‚úÖ No paperwork hassles
- ‚úÖ Close deal same day

**Downside:**
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Eliminates ~40-50% of potential buyers who need financing

---

### **Option 2: Accept Financing (Expand Your Buyer Pool)**

**What to say in your listing:**
"Financing welcome - buyer responsible for securing their own financing through credit union or bank"

**How it works:**
1. Buyer gets **pre-approved** before test drive
2. You verify pre-approval letter
3. Buyer's bank cuts check directly to you
4. You sign over title once you have the check

**Benefits:**
- ‚úÖ More potential buyers (40-50% more)
- ‚úÖ You still get paid in full immediately
- ‚úÖ You're not responsible for financing - buyer handles it

**Your requirements:**
- Must have **pre-approval letter** before test drive
- Must be able to close within 3-5 business days
- Check comes from bank/credit union directly to you

---

### **Option 3: Recommend Specific Lenders (Best of Both Worlds)**

**Add this to your listing:**

"Financing available through:
- PenFed Credit Union (apply online)
- LightStream by SunTrust
- Your local credit union

Buyer must have pre-approval before test drive."

**Benefits:**
- ‚úÖ Helps serious buyers know where to go
- ‚úÖ Speeds up process
- ‚úÖ You still get cash immediately
